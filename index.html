Our Story

Queen was born from a passion for empowering women through elegant, high-quality accessories. Our founder, inspired by the strength and grace of queens throughout history, set out to create a brand that celebrates feminine power in all its forms.

Each piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed to help you express your unique style and confidence. We believe that every woman deserves to feel like royalty in her own skin.

Help Center

Shipping Information

We offer shipping across Egypt with the following options:

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days) - EGP 30
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days) - EGP 60
  • Free shipping on orders over EGP 1000

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. You can return or exchange items within 14 days of delivery.

Conditions for returns:

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Tags must still be attached
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer

Size Guide

Our accessories are designed to fit most women comfortably. Here's our general sizing guide:

  • Necklaces: Adjustable chains from 40cm to 50cm
  • Earrings: Standard post or hook sizes
  • Hair Accessories: One size fits most

Care Instructions

To keep your Queen accessories looking their best:

  • Store in a dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid contact with water, perfumes, and lotions
  • Clean with a soft, dry cloth
  • Remove before swimming or showering

FAQs

Q: How can I track my order?

A: Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, Vodafone Cash, and cash on delivery.

Q: Can I modify or cancel my order?
